How to use AgriQ Pro 🚀

Welcome to the ultimate preparation terminal! Here is your complete guide to mastering the app and crushing your exams.

The core of your preparation! On the Dashboard, look for the Study Module section.
  • Tap Choose Subject to open the dropdown menu.
  • Select an available subject (modules marked as LIVE are active today).
  • Tap Start Session to begin your practice.
  • Once you complete a module, it will be marked as COMPLETED so you know exactly where you stand!
Boost your speed and accuracy every single day!

Tap the green Daily Rapid Drill banner on your Dashboard. You'll get exactly 10 fresh questions and only 5 minutes to solve them. Your scores here directly affect your special Daily Drill Rank!
Welcome to the elite training ground.

By tapping the purple COAK Monks banner, you enter a specialized module with its own unique leveling system. As you complete these advanced tests, you earn Monk XP and rank up your Monk Level. Perfect for those wanting to push beyond the basics!
Every correct answer gives you XP (Experience Points).
  • Global Rank: Your overall standing among all students across all standard study modules.
  • Daily Rank: Your ranking based specifically on your performance in the Daily Rapid Drills.
Tap on the rank cards at the top of the Dashboard to view the full leaderboard and see how you stack up against the competition!
Want to stand out on the Leaderboard?

Tap the pencil icon in the top right corner of the Dashboard to update your official Display Name. You can also view your full stats by tapping the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.
Pro Tip: AgriQ Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it directly to your home screen from your browser menu (Look for "Add to Home Screen").

If we release new features, a dark "New Version Available!" banner will slide up from the bottom of your screen. Just tap UPDATE NOW to get the latest content instantly!
